Updating the location of a taxi

Guidelines

We recommend an always accessible on/off button with the logo le.taxi that will trigger if the location is send or not to le.taxi.

Obligations

You must update the location of a taxi at least every 5 seconds, at must every second.
A taxi must be able to stop to send its location to le.taxi.
